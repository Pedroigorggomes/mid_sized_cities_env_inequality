# IBGE base aggregates (Demography / Dwellings)
v0001         | integer  | persons         | Total persons residing in the tract (IBGE, 2022). Denominator for race/colour percentages.
v0002         | integer  | dwellings       | Total dwellings = DPPO + DPPV + DPPUO + DPIO + DCCM + DCSM (IBGE definitions).
v0003         | integer  | dwellings       | Total private dwellings = DPPO + DPPV + DPPUO + DPIO.
v0004         | integer  | dwellings       | Total collective dwellings = DCCM + DCSM.
v0005         | float    | persons/dwell.  | Average residents in occupied private dwellings = (Total persons in DPO) / (DPPO + DPIO).
v0006         | float    | %               | Percentage of imputed occupied private dwellings = 100 × (Imputed DPO / Total DPO). Range 0–100.
v0007         | integer  | dwellings       | Total occupied private dwellings (DPPO + DPIO). Denominator for P_Agua, P_Esgo, P_Lixo.

Notes on IBGE acronyms (Portuguese, as per IBGE):
- DPPO = Domicílios Particulares Permanentes Ocupados (occupied, permanent)
- DPPV = Domicílios Particulares Permanentes Vagos (vacant, permanent)
- DPPUO = Domicílios Particulares Permanentes de Uso Ocasional (occasional use, permanent)
- DPIO = Domicílios Particulares Improvisados Ocupados (occupied, improvised)
- DCCM = Domicílios Coletivos com Moradores (collective with residents)
- DCSM = Domicílios Coletivos sem Moradores (collective without residents)

# Race/colour counts (IBGE)
Brancos       | integer  | persons         | Self-declared white (V01317). Count.
Pretos        | integer  | persons         | Self-declared black (V01318). Count.
Amarelos      | integer  | persons         | Self-declared yellow/Asian (V01319). Count.
Pardos        | integer  | persons         | Self-declared brown/mixed (V01320). Count.
Indigena      | integer  | persons         | Self-declared Indigenous (V01321). Count.
V01322        | integer  | persons         | Race/colour undeclared / ignored (V01322). Count.

# Percentages derived in this repository
P_Branca      | float    | %               | White (%) = 100 × V01317 / v0001.
P_Preta       | float    | %               | Black (%) = 100 × V01318 / v0001.
P_Amarela     | float    | %               | Yellow/Asian (%) = 100 × V01319 / v0001.
P_Parda       | float    | %               | Brown/mixed (%) = 100 × V01320 / v0001.
P_Indigena    | float    | %               | Indigenous (%) = 100 × V01321 / v0001.

# Basic services (percentages derived in this repository)
P_Agua        | float    | %               | Dwellings with piped water (general distribution network) (%) = 100 × V00111 / v0007.
P_Esgo        | float    | %               | Dwellings with sewage via general/pluvial network (%) = 100 × (V00309 + V00310) / v0007. (Fallback: V00309 only if V00310 is missing.)
P_Lixo        | float    | %               | Dwellings with household waste collected by public service (%) = 100 × V00397 / v0007.

# Other indicators
TX_Alf_15     | float    | %               | Literacy rate of population aged 15+ (0–100). Source: IBGE.
id_setor      | string   | –               | Copy of CD_SETOR for internal joins (1:1 with CD_SETOR).
RpC_2010      | float    | BRL/person      | Household per capita income (2010) harmonised to the 2022 tract grid via area-weighting (2010×2022 overlay, Brazil Albers). See Script 02.
